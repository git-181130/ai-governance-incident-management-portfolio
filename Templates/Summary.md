# Governance Templates and Operational Tools

Standardized Documentation and Control Instruments

---

## Overview

This folder contains standardized templates and operational tools used to support consistent, high-quality execution of AI governance, incident management, and risk oversight activities.

These templates ensure uniform documentation, accountability, and audit readiness across all stages of the AI system lifecycle.

They translate governance frameworks and methodologies into practical working instruments.

---

## Purpose

The primary objectives of these templates are to:

* Standardize incident and risk documentation
* Improve analytical consistency
* Strengthen accountability and ownership
* Support regulatory and audit review
* Reduce operational variability

They enable scalable and repeatable governance operations.

---

## Template Portfolio

The following standardized templates are maintained.

---

### Incident Report Template

Purpose
To document detection, impact, response, and resolution of AI system incidents.

Key Elements

* Incident classification and severity
* Impact assessment
* Containment actions
* Stakeholder communication
* Resolution summary

---

### Root Cause Analysis Template

Purpose
To structure systematic investigation of contributing factors and failures.

Key Elements

* Problem statement
* Causal chain analysis
* Contributing conditions
* Evidence references
* Corrective action plan

---

### Risk Update Template

Purpose
To integrate incident findings into enterprise risk management systems.

Key Elements

* Updated risk classification
* Likelihood and impact scoring
* Control enhancements
* Residual risk assessment
* Approval records

---

### Governance Review Template

Purpose
To document executive and committee-level oversight activities.

Key Elements

* Review agenda
* Findings summary
* Decisions and approvals
* Resource allocations
* Follow-up actions

---

### Maturity Assessment Template

Purpose
To evaluate governance capability and organizational resilience over time.

Key Elements

* Capability indicators
* Performance trends
* Control effectiveness ratings
* Improvement targets
* Review outcomes

---

## Usage Standards

All templates are used in accordance with defined governance procedures.

Usage standards include:

* Mandatory completion for relevant activities
* Evidence attachment requirements
* Version control and approval tracking
* Secure storage and retention policies

Incomplete or informal documentation is not accepted.
