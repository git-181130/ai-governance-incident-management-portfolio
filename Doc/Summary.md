
# Strategic Governance Documentation

AI Regulatory Alignment, Operating Model, and Maturity Roadmap

---

## Overview

This folder contains strategic governance documents that define the long-term structure, regulatory alignment, and capability development model for AI-enabled systems.

These materials extend beyond operational incident management and provide an architectural view of how AI governance is embedded into organizational design.

They represent the strategic layer of the AI governance portfolio.

---

## Purpose

The documents in this folder are designed to:

* Align AI operations with regulatory and legal obligations
* Define governance accountability structures
* Establish oversight and decision-making models
* Guide long-term capability development
* Support executive and regulator-level discussions

They complement the operational frameworks and case studies contained elsewhere in the repository.
